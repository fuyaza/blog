Making Mistakes

June 10, 2026

To a certain extent, we are all afraid to make mistakes, be it in stuides or in life in general.

But then I remember that the earlier we make mistakes, the earlier we learn. That’s how we figure out what works and what doesn’t, so we can make better decisions in the future.

Note that, of course, this is not a justification to intentionally make mistakes.
